Sourcing

Where Our Coffee Comes From

We believe great coffee starts at the source. Every bean is traceable.

Ethiopia

The birthplace of coffee. We source single-origin beans from Yirgacheffe and Sidamo — known for their bright, floral complexity.

The Craft

Our Roasting Process

Every batch is roasted with intention and precision.

01

Green Selection

We hand-select green beans from our partner farms, grading for size, density, and moisture content.

02

Profile Development

Each origin gets a custom roast profile designed to highlight its unique flavor characteristics.

03

Small-Batch Roasting

We roast in 10-pound batches on our Probat roaster, monitoring temperature curves in real time.

04

Cupping & QC

Every batch is cupped by our team before packaging. If it doesn't meet our standard, it doesn't ship.
